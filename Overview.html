Why is air pollution needs to be noticed?

One of the biggest risks to the public's health nowadays is the problem of air pollution. The spread of air pollution poses a serious threat to everyone's health, including that of those who live in cities and isolated places. In this instance, air pollution is a massive issue that affects the entire nation.
